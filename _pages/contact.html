Get in Touch

To inquire about collaborating, or getting our help for your projects that are related to creating high-resolution 3D body-scanned objects, animated models, and virtual garment prototyping, please email us.

Want to join the Lab?

Graduate and undergraduate students as well as postdoctoral researchers interested in joining the Lab are encouraged to contact us to discuss possibilities and apply to the Department of Human Centered Design at Cornell University.

Fashion & Body Tech Lab is located at 112 Human Ecology Building, 37 Forest Home Dr. Ithaca, NY 14853.
